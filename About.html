About Us

CJR Hospital was founded in the year 2000 by the Founding Fathers Sir.Clive D'szoua, Sir.James D'souza and Sir.Rehan Fargose,it was founded as a part of their vision to provide good healthcare and medical facilities to the people of Borivali(T.E mini project),Since 20 years we have been continuosly serving the people of Borivali for every day and night and will continue to do so to fulfill the vision of our founders.

We have been Borivali's leading healthcare instituion for decades now,with unparalleled excellence due to higly trained and experienced in-house staff that is yet to be matched.Our motto is:"A heritage in care. A reputation in excellence."

What We Do

We provide treatment for Phsyical wounds/injuries,Cardiovascular,Neurological,Pysiological as well as EMT services. We also provide housing for the realtives of the patients who live far away from the hospital
